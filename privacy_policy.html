Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy describes how this tool collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our kanban board application (the "Service").


1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Service. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account and Profile Information: When you register for an account, we collect personal data such as your name and email address. You may also choose to provide additional information, like a profile picture or other details.
  • User-Provided Content: We store and process content you create and upload to the Service, including tasks, task descriptions, comments, checklists, attachments, and any other information you add to the boards. This content may contain personal data about you or others.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us for support, we may keep a record of that communication, including your email address and any information you provide.
  • Automatically Collected Information: When you use the Service, we automatically collect certain technical information about your device and usage. This may include:
    • Log Data: Information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, the pages you visit, and the time and date of your use.
    • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., web beacons, pixels) to recognize you, analyze usage patterns, and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate and provide our Service, manage your account, process payments, and provide customer support.
  • To Improve the Service: To analyze how the Service is used, understand user preferences, and develop new features.
  • For Security: To protect the Service, prevent fraud, and enforce our terms of use.
  • For Communication: To send you service-related notifications, updates, and other relevant information. You can manage your notification preferences in your account settings.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade your personal data. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: Your name, profile picture, and the content you post on boards may be visible to other members of the boards you are a part of.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data storage, payment processing, and analytics. These providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights or property, or ensure the safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use measures like data encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
